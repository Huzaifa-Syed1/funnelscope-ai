# FunnelScope AI — v2.0

Turn your funnel data into revenue insights.

FunnelScope AI acts like a **Chartered Accountant for your business funnel** — analyzing where users drop off, how much revenue you're losing, and exactly what to fix.

---

## 💡 What it does

- 📉 Detects funnel bottlenecks instantly  
- 💰 Quantifies revenue loss from conversion drops  
- ⚡ Suggests high-ROI fixes based on real data  
- 🧠 Includes an AI Chartered Accountant for strategic guidance  
- 📊 Interactive 3D funnel visualization  

---

## 🚨 Example Insight

> “You’re losing **80% at checkout**, costing ~$17,000/month.  
> Fixing this step alone could increase revenue by **2–3x**.”

---

## 🧠 Why this matters

Most tools show data.

FunnelScope tells you:
- what it means  
- what to fix  
- and what impact it will have

- Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Three.js  
- Backend: Node.js (Express)
